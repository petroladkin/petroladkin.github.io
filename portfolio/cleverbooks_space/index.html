App description
Get ready to explore space and engineering missions with your mobile device! CleverBooks Space app engages students to explore the Solar System, Rocket Building and Outer Space in an interactive way through 3D visualization. Pair the app with CleverBooks physical products to let K1 to K-6 students assemble a rocket and lead their own space mission to Mercury, Earth, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune through Augmented Reality experience.

The content is based on global curriculum guidelines and recommended as a supplementary material to teach STEM concepts for the primary school students. Adaptable to multiple STEM subjects (engineering, astronomy, space exploration) and grades, CleverBooks Space App allows students to immerse in the learning experience of our galaxy and bring space travel to life through unique digital experience. To use the CleverBooks Space App you need a mobile device (phone or tablet) with a camera at the back.

The space journey Include:

  • Solar System Mode
  • Space rocket assembling/ interactive puzzle
  • Space missions to different planets
